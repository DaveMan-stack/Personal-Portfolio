Coding Journey

David is a 20-year-old teenager who has a great passion for coding. His journey into the world of programming began when he was around 15 to 16 years old. At that age, his mother gifted him a computer after he performed exceptionally well on a public exam.

Initially, like any other kid, David was just using his computer to watch movies on YouTube and play video games. However, one day while browsing through YouTube, he stumbled upon a video of a twelve-year-old boy who had mastered two programming languages and was creating apps. This was the turning point for David, and he became intrigued by the idea of coding.

David started his coding journey by learning Python, HTML, and CSS. As time went by, he progressed to learning Django and other advanced coding languages. By the time he turned 19, he had already mastered two programming languages, Python and JavaScript

Now, David is proficient in various frameworks and technologies such as React and Tailwind CSS, which makes him a versatile coder. His passion for coding has not only given him a sense of purpose but also inspired him to pursue a career in computer science.

In addition to mastering various programming languages and frameworks, David has also put his skills to use by creating numerous projects. He has developed a content management system with Django, a to-do list app with Django, and another to-do list app with JavaScript.

Apart from these projects, David has also taken up several coding challenges, which have helped him refine his skills and learn new things. He has a strong desire to constantly improve and expand his knowledge base.

Currently, David is working on creating his own personal portfolio website, which will showcase his skills and projects. He understands the importance of having a strong online presence, and this project is one of his top priorities. Through his hard work and dedication, David has proven himself to be a talented and motivated coder.

Content Management System

This was a follow through projects on YouTube. It's so comprehensive that it covers all the core concepts of Django, ranging from UserRegistration, UserRoles & Permission, Login & Logout, Search Functionality, to Create Update Delete (CRUD) and So much more. It was offered by Dennis Ivy.
After I've finished the whole project I created a cloned version myself. And this is the cloned version.

The projects is almost like an ecommerce website. It's has two performa, that of the Admin which is also the owner of the business and the customers which are also users of the websites. In the admin interface we can see all of the customers of that business and all their information such as their name email phone and all their orders. The functionality we can create one or multiple orders for a particular customer, update them or delete them. Customers can also login to their profile which cantainer a profile picture that they have upload and all their orders. There is also a settings interface that can enable them to customized their information. They themselves can place an order.
